Your Hifdh System منهج الحفظ
The Core Rule: Never move to a new ayah until the current one is completely locked. Minimum 50 repetitions from memory before moving on.

Daily Session — 45 Minutes

1
Recall — No Mushaf (10 min)
Recite yesterday's ayahs from pure memory. Never look first. Fail, look once, try again. The struggle is what builds the neural pathway.
10 min
2
New Memorization (25 min)
3 ayahs per session. Read one ayah several times looking. Close mushaf. Attempt from memory. Count 30 repetitions minimum before moving on. Update your position after the session.
25 min
3
Review Rotation (10 min)
Go to REVIEW tab. Recite each page from memory. Mark honestly. Red = every day. Amber = weekly. Green = monthly. The app schedules everything automatically.
10 min
Status Guide:
🟢 Solid — Full page, no mistakes, no hesitation → review monthly
🟡 Decent — Mostly correct, minor hesitation → review weekly
🔴 Needs Work — Gaps or uncertainty → review every day
🔵 Familiar — Know by ear, not formally memorized (Al-Kahf)
The Non-Negotiable: Never miss a day. Even 10 minutes and one ayah. The streak is everything.
When you finish a page:
1. Go to Progress tab → tap surah → tap page → mark 🟡 Amber
2. Update your position in Today tab to the next page/ayah
That's it — the page automatically enters the revision cycle.
