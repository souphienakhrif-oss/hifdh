Your Hifdh System منهج الحفظ
The Core Rule: Never move to a new ayah until the current one is completely locked. Minimum 50 repetitions from memory before moving on. This is what actually works.

Daily Session — 40 Minutes

1
Recall — No Mushaf (10 min)
Recite yesterday's ayahs from pure memory. Never look first. The struggle to recall is what builds the neural pathway. Fail, look once, try again without looking.
10 min
2
New Memorization (20 min)
3 ayahs on normal days, up to 5 on strong days. Read one ayah several times looking. Close mushaf. Attempt from memory. 50 total attempts before moving to next ayah. Update your position after each session.
20 min
3
Review Rotation (15 min)
Red pages every day. Amber pages weekly. Green pages monthly. The app calculates which pages need attention today. Go to the REVIEW tab. Recite each page from memory and mark honestly.
15 min
4
Listening (5 min)
Close your eyes. Listen to Sheikh Mishary or Sheikh Al-Husary recite today's new ayahs 1–2 times. Trains your ear to correct melody and pronunciation.
5 min
Status Guide:
🟢 Solid — Full page, no mistakes, no hesitation. Review monthly.
🟡 Decent — Mostly correct, minor hesitation. Review weekly.
🔴 Needs Work — Gaps or uncertainty. Review every single day.
🔵 Familiar — Know it by ear, never formally memorized (Al-Kahf).
The Non-Negotiable: Never miss a day. Even 10 minutes and one ayah. The streak is everything. Breaking consistency for 3 days costs more in review time than the days missed.
